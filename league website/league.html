Introduction

What is League of Legends?

  • League of legends is a team based MOBA that is free to play and fun for everyone.
  • It has been the most played PC game in North America and Europe for quite some time since 2012.
  • The game is available on Windows, MAC, and Mobile!
  • With updates every two weeks, the game doesn't seem to be losing popularity anytime soon.
  • Founded by Riot Games in 2009
    • Maps

    There are two main maps available to play on, those being Summoner's Rift and Howling Abyss.

    Summoner's Rift is a 5v5 type map that includes 3 lanes and 2 jungles to roam around, where the main objective is to eliminate the nexus of the opponent's base. This is the main map, and involves strategic plans to win.

    Howling Abyss is also a 5v5 type map, but is only one lane, and no jungle. This is a fun way to play with your team, with no strategy really needed.

    Riot Games often releases game modes with other maps, but they are limited for their time.

    Runes

    Runes are enhancements that add abilities and buffs to the champion. The player can choose their loadout of runes before the match begins. There are 5 classes of runes, and players can select one main (with a strong keystone ability) and one subset.

  • Precision

    • Precision is mainly used for champions that attack a lot. Marksman and fighters can be seen running these runes a lot.

  • Domination

    • Domination is, as it says, a dominating rune. Champions that want to kill with quick bursts of damage will use these runes.

  • Sorcery

    • Sorcery is involved in a lot of spell casting. Mages and supports will be using sorcery runes.

  • Resolve

    • Resolve is a rune tree dedicated to solid tanks. Champions that last long in fights, but deal less damage will find these runes useful.

  • Inspiration

    • Inspiration runes are tricky, but they are still viable. Special champions with specific playstyles can abuse inspiration runes to its best, but supports can also use them too.

    Summoner Spells

    Players are given 2 summoner spells each for their champion. These abilities open the door to creative plays.

    There are 12 summoner spells to choose from -

    Heal - an auto-targeted summoner spell that instantly restores health to your champion and an ally while also granting a small burst of movement speed.

    Ghost - a self-targeted summoner spell that increases your champion's movement speed and grants them the ability to ignore unit collision for a few seconds.

    Barrier - a self-targeted summoner spell that shields your champion from damage for a few seconds.

    Exhaust - a unit-targeted summoner spell that slows an enemy champion's movement speed and reduces their damage dealt for a few seconds.

    Mark / Dash - a summoner spell exclusive to the Howling Abyss icon Howling Abyss. Mark uses direction-targeting while Dash uses auto-targeting. It throws a snowball that, upon impact, deals true damage and over the next few seconds, the spell may be reactivated to dash towards the marked target to deal the same damage.
